    Renaissance
  • Renaissance is a Secular, Non-Profit and Non-Political Voluntary Organization registered in January 1983, involved in development activities in the District of Pudukkottai, Tamil Nadu, for the past 40 years. Renaissance is recognized under State Law and holds several recognitions across domains of social cause.

  • The Most Notable Award is "The Best Institution" Awarded by the Government of TamilNadu On the 75th Independence Day Function By The Cheif Minister of TamilNadu.
  • Renaissance among its various activities is running a special school 'MALARCHI' created solely with the purpose of helping the cause of mentally challenged, multiply disabled and autisticChildren at Kalamavoor, near Keeranur in Pudukkottai District.

  • Malarchi Residential School of Renaissance takes care of around 75 Intellectually challenged children including 20 Orphans.

  • Renaissance offers an Individual Educational Program for every student, aimed at social and economic independence, which incorporates special education, regular academic program through trained staffs, therapeutic interventions and vocational training.

    • Also it offers speech, occupational and physiotherapy, life skills training, Yoga, computer education, music and dance, weight management and physical fitness, sports and extracurricular activities, counselling, behaviour modification, diagnostics and assessment facilities, and family counselling.


    Malarchi Special School
  • In 1990 a survey from the trust found that there were numerous children’s who are mentally challenged are left to fend themselves not only by their own parents but the society itself. These Gods Creation was in an in-human condition that no governmentorganization was there to help. So the trust stepped into the mission of helping, nurturing and caring these mentally retarded children into their hands.

  • In 1991, the “RENAISSANCE MALARCHI RESIDENTIAL SCHOOL FOR MENTALLY RETARDED CHILDREN”was started at Pudukottai, with a one-time grant of Rs.1 lakh from Tamil Nadu Government.

  • Malarchi School is a GOD send gift for the socially and economically backward parents to take care of their mentally challenged child.These parents who are below poverty lines and who are struggling to feed themselves by earning daily wages, have struggled to the core to take care of their mentally challenged child. Thus the school adopts and accommodates them with all the basic necessities and further provides education and skill development to improve their ABILITY.

OUR VISION

  1. Vision of Renaissance
  2. To provide equality and affection for theintellectually challenged children and create a society with compassion that cares them as every other normal child.

  3. Mission of Renaissance
  4. Provide state of the art rehabilitation interventions through formal education by qualified people for persons with mental retardations.

  5. Goal of Renaissance
  6. To act as a resource center for protection of rights, equal opportunity and full participation for the persons with mentally retardation.

  7. Aims & Objectives
    1. To identify and Rehabilitate the persons with Intellectually Challenged Disability.
    2. To provide the Education for the Children with Mental Retardation and Multiple Disability.
    3. To provide the Vocational Training for the persons with mental retardation.
    4. Raise the funds through donations from organizations and individuals for better assistance and purchase advanced equipment’s to serve these children
    5. To integrate the mentally handicapped children with normal children.
    6. To undertake and participate in programmes pertaining to health, education, employment, and rehabilitation of the disabled.
    7. To seek the collaboration and co-operation of other voluntary and Government agencies for development activities and programmes.
    8. To conduct and or participate in seminars, camps, conferences, research and training programmes.
    9. To aid and support deserving persons with disabilities and the aged wherever and whenever necessaries.
    10. To organise, undertake, co-ordinate guide, assist and promote integrated Rural Development approach.
    11. To help the rural innocent people in all activities and make them to participate in all existing Government programmes through our societies activities.
    12. To organise and undertake and develop Educational Institutions for needy and down trodden people, wherever and whenever necessary.
    13. To mobilize and organise the Rural Community in awareness and general education for development activities.
    14. To initiate and encourage the education of women, children and disabled on question regarding their rights and duties.
    15. To motivate and train rural mass about practicability and utility of Appropriate Technology and upgrade their capacity and skills.
